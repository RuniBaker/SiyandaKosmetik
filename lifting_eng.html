Eyelash Lifting

Eyelash lifting is a gentle technique that visually lengthens your natural lashes and gives them an impressive curl without the need for lash curlers or extensions. During the treatment, the lashes are carefully bent upwards and fixed in place, creating a denser and more voluminous appearance. The result is a striking, natural look that lasts up to six weeks, perfectly highlighting your eyes.

Eyebrow Lifting

Eyebrow lifting is an innovative treatment that shapes your eyebrows and gives them a natural lift. The hairs are gently lifted and fixed, resulting in fuller and more defined brows. This method is perfect for correcting asymmetrical eyebrows and giving the face a fresh, open expression. The results last for several weeks and provide the perfect alternative to daily styling.
